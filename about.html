About Waste Management

Waste management is the process of collecting, transporting, processing, recycling, and disposing of waste materials. Proper waste management helps protect the environment and human health.

Our Smart Waste Management project aims to create awareness about waste segregation, recycling, and responsible disposal methods. By managing waste properly, we can reduce pollution and keep our cities clean and sustainable.
